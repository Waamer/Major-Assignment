The Batman

The most recent of the three chosen movies, and also the best. Rather then a normal Batman movie where it is mostly just action, this movie also shows the detective side to Batman. The movie also makes Batman seem more 'human', full of errors and mistakes that can be seen in multiple scenes 

Tenet

An underappreciated mind boggling film that is about time traveling, thought of and represented in a whole different way then how movies usually go about showing time traveling concepts. The movie was directed by one of the best directors who was behind the production of many great movies, Christopher Nolan 

John Wick Series

The classic John Wick series has always been an all time favourite by lots of people. The non stop and iconic action from one of the most lethal assassins, John Wick, across all three films is for sure to get you hooked!
